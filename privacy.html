Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Emma is designed with privacy-first principles, ensuring your AI conversations remain completely under your control.

ðŸ”’ Key Privacy Promise

All your data stays on your device. Emma never sends your conversations, memories, or any personal information to external servers.

Data Collection & Storage

Emma collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
AI Conversations Memory preservation and search Local IndexedDB
Timestamps Chronological organization Local IndexedDB
Source URLs Context tracking Local IndexedDB
User Preferences Personalization Chrome Storage API
MTAP Metadata Protocol compliance Local IndexedDB

MTAP Protocol & Security

Emma uses the Memory Transport & Access Protocol (MTAP) which provides:

  • Content-addressed storage with cryptographic hashing
  • Local-first architecture with optional federation
  • Secure memory referencing via mtap:// addresses
  • Agent permission system for controlled access

Third-Party Services

Emma operates entirely within your browser and does not use any third-party services for:

  • Analytics or tracking
  • Data storage or processing
  • User identification
  • Advertising or marketing

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your memories in MTAP-compliant JSON format
  • Delete: Remove individual memories or clear all data
  • Disable: Turn off auto-capture at any time
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension and all associated data

Data Retention

Your memories are stored locally until you choose to delete them. Emma does not automatically expire or remove your data. The storage is limited only by your browser's quota for extensions.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as Emma evolves. Any changes will be reflected in the extension and on this page with an updated revision date.
