HML: Private, Peer‑to‑Peer Sync

The Human Memory Layer is open-source and syncs peer‑to‑peer using end‑to‑end encryption and HML Sync (WebRTC + encrypted bulletin boards). No central server can read your data.

🔐

End‑to‑End Encrypted

Your vault keys never leave your device unencrypted. Collaborators receive wrapped keys and verify with cryptographic identities.

🌐

Peer‑to‑Peer by Default

HML Sync uses browser‑native WebRTC DataChannels for direct device‑to‑device transfer, with public bulletin boards used only for encrypted signaling.

🧩

Open‑Source & Extensible

HML is open and local‑first. You can self‑host relay boards, customize transports, and build advanced CRDT sync on top.

☁️

Emma Cloud (Optional)

Choose encrypted cloud storage for always‑on availability, off‑device backup, and multi‑device presence. Tiered pricing; your data is always encrypted client‑side.
